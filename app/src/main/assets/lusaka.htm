How to Use a Ma Bus

 

1)   Know which neighbourhood your destination is in. (The app tells you the end destination of the Ma Bus route you should look for.)

 

2)   Find your Ma Bus.

 

If you are near a Ma Bus rank (a place with many parked Ma Bus vehicles or building with many Ma Buses), go there. Look for signs in the windshields that have the Ma Buss end destination.

 

If you arent near a Ma Bus rank, stand at a street corner on a busy street. When a Ma Bus approaches, raise one hand in the air out toward the street to signal you want to get on.

 

3)   Check the Ma Buss destination by asking the driver.

 

4)   Board the Ma Bus and take a seat.

 

5)   Pay for the Ma Bus at any time during your trip. Hand CASH to conductor (the person at the sliding door).

 

6)   Get off the Ma Bus by signaling to the conductor to stop when you are near your destination (follow your location on the app if you arent sure where you are).
