How to Use a Matatu

 

1)   Know which neighbourhood your destination is in. (The app tells you the end destination of the matatu route you should look for.)

 

2)   Find your matatu.

 

If you are near a matatu rank (a place with many parked matatus or building with many matatus), go there. Look for signs in the windshields that have the matatus end destination.

 

If you arent near a matatu rank, stand at a street corner on a busy street. When a matatu approaches, raise one hand in the air out toward the street to signal you want to get on.

 

3)   Check the matatus destination by asking the driver.

 

4)   Board the matatu and take a seat.

 

5)   Pay for the matatu at any time during your trip. Hand CASH to conductor (the person at the sliding door).

 

6)   Get off the matatu by signaling to the conductor to stop when you are near your destination (follow your location on the app if you arent sure where you are).
