How to Use a Minibus Taxi

 

1)   Know which neighbourhood your destination is in. (The app tells you the end destination of the taxi route you should look for.)

 

2)   Find your taxi.

 

If you are near a taxi rank (a place with many parked taxis or building with many taxis), go there. Look for signs in the windshields that have the taxis end destination.

 

If you arent near a taxi rank, stand at a street corner on a busy street. When a taxi approaches, raise one hand in the air out toward the street to signal you want to get on.

 

3)   Check the taxis destination by asking the driver.

 

4)   Board the taxi and take a seat.

 

5)   Pay for the taxi at any time during your trip. Hand CASH to conductor (the person at the sliding door).

 

6)   Get off the taxi by signaling to the conductor to stop when you are near your destination (follow your location on the app if you arent sure where you are).
